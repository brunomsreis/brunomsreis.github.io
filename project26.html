Real Estate Photography @ ORM

As part of my work at ORM, I was invited to photograph one of the new apartments recently added to our real estate portfolio. The goal was to capture the character and spatial qualities of the property, highlighting its architecture, natural light, and interior atmosphere. This project allowed me to apply a professional and aesthetic approach to real estate photography, balancing clarity, composition, and visual appeal to create images that would support the marketing and promotional strategy of the apartment.
